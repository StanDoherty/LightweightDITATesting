Remote Lighting Network Overview

The Remote Lighting Network kit allows you to operate network-based home lighting through a remote control

The Remote Lighting Network kit includes a wireless smart lighting system that helps make the lighting in your home more energy efficient and easier to manage. The kit includes the following components:

Remote Control

Allows you to power on, power off, and dim groups of lights on your network.

LED Light Bulbs

Energy-efficient network light bulbs you can install into standard light fixtures.

Electrical hazards can cause burns, shocks and electrocution (death).
